## 1. Purpose of the Pilot

The purpose of this paid pilot is to **deploy and evaluate HyperAgency in a real operational environment** and to determine whether it provides sufficient value to justify continued use, licensing, or further engagement.

This pilot is **not a demo, trial, or guarantee of a production-ready system**, but a time-boxed collaborative evaluation.

---

## 2. Scope of the Pilot

During the pilot, the Provider will:

* Deploy HyperAgency into the Client’s environment (or Provider-hosted environment, as agreed)
* Implement **up to 1–2 autonomous agents or workflows** addressing a defined operational use case
* Collaborate directly with the Client to iterate toward functional reliability
* Provide architectural and operational guidance related to agent deployment

**Out of scope unless explicitly agreed in writing:**

* Full automation guarantees
* Enterprise-grade hardening
* Custom feature development beyond pilot needs
* 24/7 support or on-call availability
